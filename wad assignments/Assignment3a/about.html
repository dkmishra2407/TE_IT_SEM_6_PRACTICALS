About Mountain Adventures

We've been guiding mountain adventures for over 20 years

Our Story

Mountain Adventures began in 2004 when a group of passionate hikers decided to share their love of the mountains with others. What started as a small guiding service has grown into a full-service adventure company.

Today, we offer a wide range of experiences from day hikes to multi-week expeditions, comfortable cabin stays, and educational programs about mountain ecosystems.

Our Mission

We believe that connecting with nature is essential for human wellbeing. Our mission is to provide safe, educational, and inspiring mountain experiences that foster a deep appreciation for the natural world.

We are committed to sustainable tourism practices and work actively to preserve the mountain environments we love.
